# 🚗 Mission 1: Wake Up Your Cutebot!

## 📖 Story

Your Cutebot has arrived, but it still does not know how to move.

Your job is to teach it some basic driving skills and help it complete its first journey.

---

## 🎯 Mission Goal

Program your Cutebot to move around and complete a simple journey.

---

## 🧠 What you will learn

- How to control motors
- How to change speed
- How to create a sequence of instructions
- How to test and improve your code

---

## 🧰 Equipment

- 🤖 Cutebot
- 🟦 micro:bit
- 💻 [MakeCode](https://makecode.microbit.org/_HoyXTX7eUJ0j)

---

## 🚀 Starter Challenge: First Drive

Can you make your Cutebot:

- Move forwards
- Stop
- Move backwards

Try changing the speed.

What happens when you make the robot go faster or slower?

---

## ⭐ Bronze Mission: The Straight Line

Can you make your Cutebot:

- Travel from the start line to the finish line
- Stop exactly at the finish

Things to try:

- Faster speed
- Slower speed
- Different timings

💡 Tip: small changes can make a big difference.

---

## ⭐⭐ Silver Mission: The Robot Dance

Can you program your Cutebot to create a dance routine?

Try making it:

1. Move forwards
2. Turn left
3. Move forwards
4. Turn right

Can you create your own robot dance?

---

## ⭐⭐⭐ Gold Mission: Figure of 8 Challenge

Can you make your Cutebot drive in a figure of 8?

Things to think about:

- How long should each turn last?
- Should both motors have the same speed?
- How can you make your turns smoother?

💡 Robot engineers test, change, and test again.

---

## 🔍 Extension Mission: Follow the Line

Now make your robot smarter!

Create a track using black tape.

Can your Cutebot follow the line?

---

## 🧠 New Skills

You will use:

- Line tracking sensors
- `if` statements
- Repeating loops

---

## 💻 Challenge Idea

Your robot needs to make decisions:

```text
IF the left sensor sees black
    turn left
IF the right sensor sees black
    turn right
OTHERWISE
    drive forwards
```

---

## 🏆 Ultimate Challenge: Robot Maze

Create your own track.

Can another team:

- Complete your course?
- Beat your time?
- Improve your code?

---

## 🎉 Mission Complete!

You have taught your Cutebot how to move.

Next mission: make your robot smarter!
